About Snack & Stream

Snack & Stream is a cozy movie and TV discovery website made for people who want to find something fun to watch without scrolling forever.

Inspired by the feeling of grabbing snacks before a movie night, our site helps users explore trending titles, search for shows, save favorites, and get recommendations based on their mood.

What You Can Do Here

  • Explore trending movies and shows.
  • Search for titles that match your interests.
  • Take a mood quiz to get a recommendation.
  • Save shows to your personal dashboard.
